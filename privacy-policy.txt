Privacy Policy for Conditional Discount App

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Conditional Discount ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you install and use our Shopify application.

2. Information We Collect
- Store Information: Store name, domain, and email address
- Discount Configuration: Settings you create for discount rules
- Product Data: Product IDs and titles used in discount rules

3. How We Use Information
We use collected information solely to:
- Provide discount functionality in your store
- Apply automatic discounts at checkout
- Save and manage your discount rules

4. Data Storage
- Discount rules are stored securely in our database
- We do not store customer personal information
- We do not store payment or credit card data

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties except:
- When required by law
- To provide app functionality through Shopify's platform

6. Data Retention
We retain your data while the app is installed. Upon uninstallation, your discount rules and settings are deleted.

7. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

8. Your Rights
You may request to:
- Access your stored data
- Delete your data
- Export your data

9. Contact
For privacy questions, contact: support@masum-billah.com

10. Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
